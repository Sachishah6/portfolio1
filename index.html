What I Do

Services &
Expertise

From concept to camera to caption — I handle the full creative lifecycle of social media content, with a focus on short-form video, brand storytelling, community growth, and event-led campaigns.

01

Collection & Exhibition Reels

Script and produce visually-driven reels to launch new collections — like Navratri and Summer exhibitions — with high-energy hooks designed to capture immediate attention and drive strong organic discovery.

02

Full Creative Lifecycle

Lead end-to-end production of short-form video: from initial storyboarding and shoot direction through advanced editing, audio syncing, and final delivery — built for high-impact launches.

03

Account & Content Management

Manage social accounts and content calendars — planning and executing engaging posts and reels while building a consistent brand presence through creative storytelling and active audience engagement.

04

Event-Based Campaigns

Collaborate with teams to promote pickleball and paddleball events, contribute to on-ground marketing, and generate innovative promotional ideas to enhance reach, sign-ups, and participation.

05

Sponsorship & Partnerships

Assist in securing sponsorships through targeted outreach and presentation support — strengthening brand partnerships and expanding visibility across sporting and lifestyle communities.

06

Brand Storytelling

Translate brand values into compelling visual narratives across feed posts, reels, and stories — maintaining aesthetic coherence while keeping content fresh, relatable, and growth-oriented.
